I am a PhD student at the School of Computing at the University of Eastern Finland and a member of the [Learning Analytics research group](https://sites.uef.fi/learning-analytics). My research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence (AI) in education, specifically, I work on agent-driven learning platforms that aim to address challenges in current AI-driven learning systems and improve human-AI interactions in learning environments.

I holds a Master’s degree from Azarbaijan Shahid Madani University, Iran, and I has over six years of professional experience as a software engineer, with strong expertise in programming, software design, system architecture, and DevOps practices. Before joining the Learning Analytics group, I worked as Chief Technology Officer at [Appetit](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fit.appetit&hl=en), where I led the technical team and supervised software development processes.
